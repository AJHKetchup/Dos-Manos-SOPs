# Brand Resource Checklist

Status: Draft helper checklist
Last updated: 2026-07-03

Use with `04-Brand-and-Resource-Usage-SOP.md`.

## Copy check

- [ ] Uses care, clear communication, organized loading, customer-property protection, reliable service, and fair value.
- [ ] Sounds practical, local, and human.
- [ ] Avoids cheapest-mover positioning.
- [ ] Avoids fake urgency.
- [ ] Avoids unsupported guarantees.
- [ ] Avoids unverified claims about licensing, insurance, piano moving, antiques, storage, same-day availability, specialty services, or territory/location status.
- [ ] Uses broad service-area language for public copy unless management approves a specific listing context.
- [ ] Does not change referral, compensation, legal, pricing, insurance, or public claims.

## Media check

- [ ] Real crew/truck/job proof is preferred over stock.
- [ ] Image/video is not blurry, dark, unsafe, or confusing.
- [ ] No customer paperwork, screens, payment info, labels, or private documents visible.
- [ ] No children’s faces.
- [ ] No house numbers, license plates, apartment numbers, or private location details.
- [ ] No customer-sensitive belongings or conditions that could embarrass/identify the customer.
- [ ] Original asset source is recorded.

## Approval check

- [ ] Public GBP, website, social, ad, directory, review reply, and media use is owner-approved before publishing.
- [ ] Unclear claims or assets are routed for review instead of used live.
