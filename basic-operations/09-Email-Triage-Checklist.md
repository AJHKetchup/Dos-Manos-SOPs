# Email Triage Checklist

Status: Draft helper checklist
Last updated: 2026-07-03

Use with `02-Email-Triage-SOP.md`.

## Start here

- [ ] Review unread inbox / `Action Needed` first.
- [ ] Identify queue: lead, customer/job, Supermove, payment, COI/insurance, claim/refund/damage, GBP/review, system log, marketing/list, or old/no-action.
- [ ] Apply or confirm the correct plain-language label.
- [ ] Keep action items visible until handled.
- [ ] Draft internal next action if useful.

## Keep visible / manual review

- [ ] Leads and quote requests.
- [ ] Customer communication.
- [ ] Supermove/job/scheduling issues.
- [ ] Claims, refunds, damage, complaints, legal, ICC, chargeback, or review threats.
- [ ] QuickBooks, Intuit, Stripe, payment, bank, fraud, accounting, tax, or payroll notices.
- [ ] COI, insurance, certificate, or compliance messages.
- [ ] GBP, Google, Maps, reviews, testimonials, or platform health.
- [ ] Attachments that may contain business records.

## Do not do without approval

- [ ] Send/reply/forward.
- [ ] Unsubscribe.
- [ ] Archive in bulk.
- [ ] Mark read/unread in bulk.
- [ ] Delete/trash.
- [ ] Create/change persistent filters.
- [ ] Expose raw sensitive message content outside approved restricted storage.
